About

ROS Agriculture is an open source community that focuses on empowering farmers with robotic tools. We are working together with education, industry and governments to promote agricultural robotics.

Whether you're a student looking to learn about robotics, a professional looking to showcase your work, or a farmer with a project, we would love to hear from you.
